ABOUT US

I AM GOD is an interactive sound-based installation that uses an AI-trained keyword detection system to recognize the words GOD, CONTROL, and ONE. Each spoken keyword triggers a corresponding animation, allowing the audience’s voice to directly shape the visual experience.

Conceptually, the project satirizes the chaos of today’s political climate, where entitled political figures often act as if they hold godlike authority while avoiding the real consequences of their decisions. Through the keywords, I AM GOD explores propaganda, influence, obedience, and the power of language to shape an entire nation.

This project is created by team Ngo Ai Ni in DMS3 course of the School of Design from RMIT University, mentored by Mr. Patrick Hartono and Mr. Nguyen Hoang Giang.
